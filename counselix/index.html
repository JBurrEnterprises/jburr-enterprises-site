Counselix — AI Legal Sidekick

Counselix helps you unpack confusing legal language, summarize documents, and prepare better conversations with real attorneys.

Not a law firm · Not legal advice · Built to give you clarity, not representation.

What Counselix Can Help With

  • Summarizing leases, contracts, and agreements into simple language.
  • Highlighting key terms, obligations, and potential red flags.
  • Helping you prepare questions to ask a licensed attorney.
  • Organizing multiple documents into digestible notes.

Counselix is designed for everyday people and small business owners who need more clarity than a quick internet search can provide, but who aren’t ready to retain a law firm yet.

How You’ll Use It

  • Paste or upload text (contracts, policies, emails, etc.).
  • Ask questions in plain language.
  • Get structured, easy-to-read breakdowns of what’s going on.
  • Use that clarity to talk to a real lawyer more efficiently.
