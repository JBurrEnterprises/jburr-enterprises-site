What FrontRunner Signals Does

FrontRunner Signals visualizes key price zones based on Impulse–Balance–Impulse (IBI) behavior and contextual candle structure.

  • Automatically plots demand & supply zones
  • Tracks how many times price revisits each zone
  • Assigns a strength score that degrades with overuse
  • Supports higher-timeframe IBI confirmation
  • Highlights proximity conditions for planned execution

This is a decision-support tool — not a signal spam engine.
