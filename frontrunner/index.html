What FrontRunner Signals Does

FrontRunner Signals is an invite-only TradingView indicator built around Impulse-Balance-Impulse (IBI) market structure.

It automatically identifies high-quality supply and demand zones, tracks how price interacts with those zones over time, and assigns a dynamic strength score to help traders focus only on meaningful structure.

  • Automatic IBI zone detection
  • Supply & demand zones drawn directly on chart
  • Zone retouch counter (zone decay logic)
  • Dynamic strength score (0–100)
  • Optional higher-timeframe IBI zones
  • Proximity alerts when price approaches key zones

Designed for disciplined traders who value clarity, patience, and structure.

Zone Colors Explained

  • Green: Bullish demand zones (long bias)
  • Red: Bearish supply zones (short bias)
  • Blue: Bullish compression / inside-bar structure
  • Yellow: Bearish compression / inside-bar structure

Touch Counter & Strength

Each zone displays:

Touches | Strength

Second and third touches historically provide the best reactions. Strength decays automatically as zones are retested.
