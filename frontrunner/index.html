What It Does

FrontRunner Signals highlights institutional-style price zones derived from Impulse–Balance–Impulse (IBI) behavior and related candle structure.

  • Automatic supply & demand zones
  • Retouch counting (zone durability)
  • Strength score (0–100) with decay on overuse
  • Optional higher-timeframe confirmation zones

This is a decision-support tool — not a signal service and not financial advice.

IBI in Plain English

  • Impulse — aggressive directional move
  • Balance — compression / indecision
  • Impulse Away — confirmation and zone creation

These balance areas become zones. When price revisits them later, traders watch for reactions or confirmations.
