PostPilot — AI Social Media Assistant

PostPilot helps you draft, clean up, and schedule content so you can show up consistently online without social media becoming a full-time job.

Fast · Energetic · Creator-friendly — made for people who need content, not more chaos.

What PostPilot Can Do

  • Generate post ideas from scratch or from short prompts.
  • Rewrite and clean up rough drafts into platform-ready content.
  • Help you maintain a consistent posting cadence.
  • Organize content into a simple pipeline (idea → draft → schedule).

The goal is to reduce the friction between “I should post something” and “I actually posted something that fits my brand.”

Planned Features

  • Content calendar and scheduling interface.
  • Template library for common post types.
  • Basic performance insights based on engagement metrics.
  • Multi-platform support (e.g., Instagram, X, TikTok, YouTube descriptions).
